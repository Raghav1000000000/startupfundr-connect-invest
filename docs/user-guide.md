## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Visit the StartupFundr homepage
2. Click on "Join Now" in the top navigation bar
3. Fill out the registration form with your details
4. Verify your email address through the link sent to your inbox
5. Complete your profile with additional information

### Light & Dark Mode

StartupFundr supports both light and dark modes to suit your preference:
- Toggle between modes by clicking on the sun/moon icon in the footer
- Your preference will be saved for future visits

## For Investors

### Discovering Startups

1. Navigate to the "Startups" page via the main navigation
2. Browse the available opportunities or use filters to narrow your search
3. Click on any startup card to view detailed information

### Making an Investment

1. From a startup's detail page, review all available information
2. Click the "Invest" button when you're ready
3. Enter your investment amount and review equity calculations
4. Complete the required verification steps
5. Confirm your investment

### Managing Your Portfolio

1. Go to the "Dashboard" from the main navigation
2. View the "Portfolio" section to see all your investments
3. Monitor performance metrics and updates from startups you've invested in

## For Startups

### Creating a Startup Profile

1. Register for an account and verify your email
2. Click on "Raise Capital" in the navigation
3. Complete the startup application form with company details
4. Submit required documentation for verification
5. Once approved, your startup will be listed on the platform

### Managing Your Funding Campaign

1. Access your startup dashboard from the main navigation
2. Monitor investment progress, investor communications, and metrics
3. Post updates to keep investors informed about milestones and progress

## Account Management

### Profile Settings

1. Click on your profile icon or "My Profile" in the navigation
2. Update your personal information, contact details, and preferences
3. Manage notification settings to control what updates you receive

### Security

1. Regularly update your password through the profile settings
2. Enable two-factor authentication for additional security
3. Review login history to ensure account safety

## FAQ

### For Investors

**Q: What is the minimum investment amount?**  
A: The minimum investment varies by startup but typically starts at $1,000.

**Q: How is my equity calculated?**  
A: Equity is calculated based on the startup's valuation and your investment amount.

**Q: When can I expect returns?**  
A: Returns depend on the startup's exit strategy, which could be years from investment.

### For Startups

**Q: What fees does StartupFundr charge?**  
A: StartupFundr charges a 5% success fee on funds raised.

**Q: How long does verification take?**  
A: Verification typically takes 3-5 business days.

**Q: Can I set my own equity terms?**  
A: Yes, you can set your valuation and equity offerings within platform guidelines.
